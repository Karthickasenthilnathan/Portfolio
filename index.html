About Me

My journey in tech began during my first year of engineering, where I started building a strong foundation in programming with Java, and gradually stepped into the world of web development through HTML and CSS. It was during this time that I discovered how much I enjoyed bringing ideas to life through code.

In my second semester, I became more curious about making websites interactive, which led me to learn JavaScript and React.js. Alongside that, I also picked up Python, which opened new doors to exploring automation and AI.

Now, in my third semester, I’m actively solving challenges on HackerRank and LeetCode to sharpen my problem-solving skills. I’ve also started exploring web security and Linux, while diving deeper into AI and ML technologies like OpenCV.

Beyond academics, I’m proud to serve as the Department Coordinator of Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (UBA). Through this initiative, I’ve had the opportunity to volunteer for Poonthandalam village’s wellness program — conducting career guidance sessions for government school students and participating in several household surveys. These experiences helped me grow as a person and connect technology with social impact.

Every step so far has been a mix of learning, experimenting, and growing — and I’m excited to see where this journey takes me next.
